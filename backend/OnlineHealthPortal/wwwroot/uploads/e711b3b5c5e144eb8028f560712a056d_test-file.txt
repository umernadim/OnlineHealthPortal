
## 🎤 Presentation Start (Introduction Script)

### 1. **Greeting + Hook (Attention Grabber)**
Shuruat ek relatable situation se karo:
> *“Sochiye aap ek chhote sheher mein rehte ho, aur doctor tak pohanchne ke liye ghanton ka safar karna padta hai… ya phir hospital mein line mein khade rehna padta hai. Ye problem sirf Pakistan mein nahi, duniya ke bohot se areas mein hai.”*

Ye ek **real-world pain point** highlight karega aur audience turant samajh jayegi ki tumhara project kyun relevant hai.

---

### 2. **Project Introduction (Topic)**
Ab apna project introduce karo:
> *“Isi problem ko solve karne ke liye maine ek **Online Health Consultation Portal** design kiya hai. Ye ek web application hai jahan patients apne doctors se online consult kar sakte hain, appointments book kar sakte hain, aur apni medical history securely save rakh sakte hain.”*

---

### 3. **Why It’s Needed (Problem Background)**
Short aur impactful lines:
> *“Door daraaz areas mein doctors ki kami, hospitals mein long waiting times, aur scattered medical records — ye sab problems ek patient ke liye bohot stressful hote hain. Mera portal inhi issues ka ek digital solution provide karta hai.”*

---

### 4. **Proposed Solution (One-liner)**
Ek crisp line jo tumhare project ka essence bataye:
> *“Ye portal ek single platform hai jo doctor aur patient ko connect karta hai, records ko digital banata 

👉 Ek sample opening line jo tum use kar sakte ho:
> *“Good morning everyone, today I’m going to present a project that addresses one of the most common challenges in healthcare — accessibility. My project is called **Online Health Consultation Portal**, and it aims to make healthcare available anytime, anywhere.”*
