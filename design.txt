**Aapke Online Health Consultation Portal project ke liye total 18-20 essential pages create karne padenge, jo patient, doctor, aur admin roles ko cover karein.** Yeh pages features jaise appointment booking, video consultation, records, etc. ko support karenge. Har role ke liye separate dashboards aur shared pages banaye jayenge.

## Common Pages
Yeh pages sab users ke liye honge:
- **Home/Landing Page**: Doctor search, quick book, features highlight.
- **Login Page**: Email/password ya role-based login.
- **Register/Signup Page**: Patient/doctor/admin ke liye alag forms.
- **Forgot Password Page**: Password recovery.
- **About Us Page**: Project info, mission (documentation ke liye).
- **Contact/Support Page**: Queries ke liye form.

## Patient Pages
Patient ke features cover karne ke liye:
- **Dashboard**: Appointments, records overview, notifications.
- **Doctor List/Search**: Filter by specialty, ratings, availability.[3]
- **Book Appointment**: Date/time select, video/chat option.
- **My Appointments**: View, cancel, reschedule list.
- **Health Records**: Upload/view history, prescriptions, reports.
- **Video/Chat Consultation**: Real-time call interface (WebRTC use karein).
- **Prescriptions**: View past ones, reminders.
- **Chat/Messages**: Doctor se messaging.
- **Profile**: Edit personal/insurance details.
- **Invoice/Bill**: Payment simulate (no real gateway).[2]

## Doctor Pages
Doctor ke functionalities ke liye:
- **Dashboard**: Upcoming appointments, patients list.
- **Patient List**: Assigned patients ka data.
- **Appointment Management**: Accept/reject, schedule.
- **Consultation Interface**: Video/chat with screen share.
- **Prescription Writer**: Online e-prescription generate.
- **Patient Records View**: Secure access to history.
- **Chat**: Patient messages handle.
- **Profile Edit**: Experience, availability update.
- **Follow-ups**: Schedule reminders.

## Admin Pages
Admin dashboard ke liye:
- **Dashboard**: Overview stats (appointments, users).
- **Manage Doctors**: Approve/add doctors, profiles.
- **Manage Patients**: Records approve, users.
- **Appointments Track**: All bookings monitor.
- **Notifications**: System-wide send.
