## 🔹 3. Tumhara Project Topic kya hai?

### ✅ **Online Health Consultation Portal (Web Application)**

Matlab:
Ek **healthcare website / web app** jahan:

* Patients online doctor se consult kar sakte hain
* Appointment book kar sakte hain
* Apni medical history save rakh sakte hain

---

## 🔹 4. Is project ki zaroorat kyun hai? (Problem Background)

Real duniya ka issue:

* Door daraaz areas mein doctors available nahi
* Hospitals mein long waiting time
* Patient ka medical record alag alag jagah scattered hota hai

👉 Solution:
Ek **single online platform** jo:

* Doctor + Patient ko connect kare
* Records digital bana de
* Time aur effort dono save kare

---

## 🔹 5. Proposed Solution (Tum kya bana rahe ho?)

Tum ek **secure web-based portal** bana rahe ho jisme:

✔ Patient:

* Doctor book kare
* Video / chat consultation kare
* Health records upload & view kare

✔ Doctor:

* Patients ka data dekhe
* Prescription likhe
* Follow-up schedule kare

✔ System:

* Reminders bheje
* Notifications show kare
* Basic AI diagnosis ka idea (simple logic level par)

---

## 🔹 6. Functional Requirements (Features kya honge?)

### 🔹 1. Appointment / Virtual Consultation

* Doctor list browse kar sakte ho
* Date & time select
* Video / audio / chat option
* Appointment cancel ya reschedule

---

### 🔹 2. Doctor Profiles

* Doctor ka naam, experience, specialty
* Ratings & reviews
* Filter (specialty, language, availability)

---

### 🔹 3. Patient Health Records

* Medical history save
* Prescriptions
* Lab reports
* Images upload (X-ray, reports etc.)
* Secure access (sirf authorized logins)

---

### 🔹 4. Video Consultation

* Secure video calling
* Screen sharing (doctor samjha sake)

---

### 🔹 5. Prescription & Follow-up

* Doctor online prescription likhe
* Patient prescription dekhe
* Medicine reminders
* Follow-up appointment

---

### 🔹 6. Chat System

* Patient doctor ko message kare
* Non-urgent queries ke liye async chat

---

### 🔹 7. Payment (Optional)

⚠ Important:

* **Real payment gateway nahi use karna**
* Sirf **invoice / bill show karna**
* Insurance details store ho sakti hain

---

### 🔹 8. Notifications & Reminders

* Appointment reminder
* Medicine reminder
* Test result update

---

### 🔹 9. Admin Dashboard

* Doctors / admin patient records manage kare
* Appointments track kare
* Records approve / update kare

---

## 🔹 8. Non-Functional Requirements (Quality points)

Matlab app:

* Fast ho
* Secure ho
* Easy to use ho
* Mobile + desktop compatible ho
* 24/7 available ho
